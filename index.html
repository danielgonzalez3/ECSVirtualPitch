Medical Diagnostic Database


About the Database

The purpose of this database is to collect information about commonly misdiagnosed diseases in order to aid medical professionals. Misdiagnosed diseases can have great effects on the outcome of treatments. That is why it is essential to correctly identify diseases as early as possible. The Medical Diagnostic Database uses machine learning on a list of symptoms to classify them into the most likely disease. This database is meant to aid professionals in their decision and is not an absolute diagnosis.
